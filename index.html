Housing Prices and Crime Grades

Project Purpose

The capstone project aimed to investigate the correlation between house pricing and crime rates, prompted by the lack of readily available crime data on real estate platforms. Using Python web scrapers, Selenium, data was gathered from crimegrade.org and redfin.com due to the absence of detailed free databases at the zip code level.
